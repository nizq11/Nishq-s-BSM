Option Pricing Tool (BSM & Black Model)

BSM Model (Stocks, FX, Interest Rates)

S = Spot price of underlying
K = Strike price
r = Risk-free interest rate (annual, decimal)
q = Dividend yield (stocks) / Foreign interest rate (FX)
σ = Volatility (annual standard deviation)
T = Time to maturity (in years)


Black Model (Options on Futures)

F = Futures price of underlying
K = Strike price
r = Risk-free interest rate
σ = Volatility of futures price
T = Time to maturity (in years)
